Stataria adds a full RPG stat and leveling system to Terraria! 

 Gain XP from dealing damage, killing enemies, and defeating bosses  
 Level up and earn stat points (1 per level)  
 Assign stats to boost your power:

VIT = +5 Max HP & regen  
STR = +1% melee damage  
AGI = +1% move/attack speed (diminishing returns)  
INT = +5 Max Mana, mana regen, +1% magic damage  
LUC = +0.5% crit chance  
END = Defense, damage reduction, status/knockback resistance, knocksback enemies (except bosses) 
POW = +1% global damage  
DEX = +1% ranged damage, ammo efficiency  
SPR = +1% summon damage, bonus minion/sentry slots (+1 minion slot every 10 points, +1 sentry every 20 points)

 XP bar appears when gaining XP  
 Draggable UI panel with reset button  
 Fully configurable stats and XP in Mod Configs

Don’t forget to set a keybind to open the Stat Panel!
