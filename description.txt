Stataria adds a full RPG stat and leveling system to Terraria! 

 Gain XP from dealing damage, killing enemies, and defeating bosses  
 Level up and earn stat points (1 per level)  
 Assign stats to boost your power:

    VIT: Increases Max Health and Regen
    STR: Increases Melee Damage 
    AGI: Increases Movement Speed and Attack Speed (diminishes after 75+ points)
    INT: Increases Mana, Regen, and Magic Damage  
    LUC: Increases Crit Chance
    END: Increases Defense, Damage Reduction (cannot reach 100%), Debuff/Knockback Resist, and Enemy Knock Back (except bosses... pretty much thorns without the damage... kind of :/)
    POW: Increases All Damages  
    DEX: Increases Ranged Damage, and Chance To Not Consume Ammo  
    SPR: Increases Summon Damage plus Minion and Sentry slots (+1 minion slot every 10 points, +1 sentry slot every 20 points) 

 XP bar appears when gaining XP  
 Draggable UI panel with reset button  
 Fully configurable stats and XP in Mod Configs
 Also now has configurable Balances!

Don’t forget to set a keybind to open the Stat Panel!
