[b] Stataria - An RPG System for Terraria [/b]

Stataria brings a stat-based leveling and character building to Terraria.

[i]Psst, hey, want a sword that you can use with this mod? Check out my XP Blade mod![/i]

[b][u] DEFAULT KEYBIND: K [/u][/b]

Gain XP by:
[list]
  [*] Dealing damage
  [*] Killing enemies
  [*] Defeating bosses (+bonus XP)
[/list]

Level up and earn [b]1 stat point per level[/b]
Assign points across 9 unique stats:
[list]
[*][b]VIT[/b]: Increases Max Health and Regen, Decreases Breath Loss Rate
[*][b]STR[/b]: Increases Melee Damage, Knockback, and Melee Armor Penetration
[*][b]AGI[/b]: Increases Movement Speed, Attack Speed, Wing Time, Jump Speed and Jump Height
  Unlocks Abilities
  [list]
    [*] Dash
    [*] Auto-Jump
    [*] Swimming
    [*] Fall Damage immunity
    [*] Teleportation (SET A KEYBIND!!!)
  [/list]
[*][b]INT[/b]: Increases Max Mana, Regen, Magic Damage, and Magic Armor Penetration, Decrease Mana Cost
[*][b]LUC[/b]: Increases Crit Chance, Luck, and Fishing Power, Decreases Enemy Aggro
[*][b]END[/b]: Increases Defense, Damage Reduction, Knockback Resistance, Enemy Knock Back (except bosses), and Enemy Aggro
[*][b]POW[/b]: Increases All Damages (but has an extremely small flat increase for Melee, Range, Magic, and Summon damage)
[*][b]DEX[/b]: Increases Ranged Damage, Chance To Not Consume Ammo, Mining Speed and Range, Block Placement Speed and Range
[*][b]SPR[/b]: Increases Summon Damage plus Minion and Sentry slots
[/list]

Simple stat panel UI (clean, draggable, has a reset stats button, and now has dynamically updating tooltips for each stat)
  
[b] Most things are configurable in the mods config so go crazy. You can make this as balanced as you want! [/b]

[b] Multiplayer is not fully tested (sometimes there are syncing problems, sometimes rejoining server fixes them), so if anything happens or problems arrise, please let me know![/b]

[b] Don’t forget to bind a key to open the Stat Panel under Controls > Keybindings.[/b]
