[b] Stataria - An RPG System for Terraria [/b]

Stataria brings a stat-based leveling and character building to Terraria.

[i]Psst, hey, want a sword that you can use with this mod? Check out my XP Blade mod![/i]

Gain XP by:
[list]
  [*] Dealing damage
  [*] Killing enemies
  [*] Defeating bosses (+bonus XP)
[/list]

Level up and earn [b]1 stat point per level[/b]
Assign points across 9 unique stats:
[list]
[*][b]VIT[/b]: Increases Max Health and Regen
[*][b]STR[/b]: Increases Melee Damage 
[*][b]AGI[/b]: Increases Movement Speed and Attack Speed (diminishes after 75+ points)
[*][b]INT[/b]: Increases Mana, Regen, and Magic Damage  
[*][b]LUC[/b]: Increases Crit Chance
[*][b]END[/b]: Increases Defense, Damage Reduction (cannot reach 100%), Debuff/Knockback Resist, and Enemy Knock Back (except bosses... pretty much thorns without the damage... kind of :/)
[*][b]POW[/b]: Increases All Damages  
[*][b]DEX[/b]: Increases Ranged Damage, and Chance To Not Consume Ammo  
[*][b]SPR[/b]: Increases Summon Damage plus Minion and Sentry slots (+1 minion slot every 10 points, +1 sentry slot every 20 points) 
[/list]

An XP bar pops up above your character when gaining XP (won't be noticable though when gaining a lot of XP... but it's there...)

Simple stat panel UI (clean, draggable, has a reset stats button, and now has dynamically updating tooltips for each stat)
  
Most stat scaling and XP values are configurable in the mod's config settings! So go crazy, or don't. Default values are what I believe is... normal?

There are now configurable balances mainly focused for multiplayer, but also affects singleplayer. (Multiplayer is not fully tested, so if anything happens or problems arrise, please let me know!)

[b] Don’t forget to bind a key to open the Stat Panel under Controls > Keybindings.[/b]
